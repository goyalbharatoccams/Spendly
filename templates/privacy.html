Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. When you use the app, we store the expense records you enter — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

We do not collect payment information, location data, or any data beyond what you explicitly enter.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling filtering by date or category.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to the core functionality of the app.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We apply industry-standard measures to protect it from unauthorised access, alteration, or disclosure.

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly uses Google Fonts to load typefaces. This request is made directly from your browser to Google's servers and is subject to Google's own privacy policy.

We do not share your expense data with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-broker services.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within 5 business days.
